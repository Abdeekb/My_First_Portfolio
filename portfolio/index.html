Flyer Design for International Programming School

This flyer design was created for the International Programming School to promote their courses and workshops. The design focuses on modern, tech-inspired visuals to attract aspiring programmers. The flyer features bold typography paired with sleek graphics that symbolize coding and innovation, such as binary patterns and abstract circuit designs. The color scheme combines shades of blue and green to represent knowledge, trust, and creativity—key elements for any educational institution. The layout is clean and structured, with clear calls-to-action for potential students to sign up for courses and events. The design aims to capture the attention of the target audience and convey the dynamic, cutting-edge nature of the school’s programming curriculum.
